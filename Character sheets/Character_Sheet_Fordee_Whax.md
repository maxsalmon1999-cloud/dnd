## Special Features
- **Favored Enemy: Humanoids** — Advantage on Survival checks to track, Intelligence checks to recall info. One additional language associated with humanoids.
- **Natural Explorer: Urban or Forest** — Difficult terrain doesn't slow the group. Can't become lost except by magic. Remain alert to danger while doing other tasks.
- **Fighting Style: Archery** — +2 to attack rolls with ranged weapons (included in longbow attack).
- **Dread Ambusher (Gloom Stalker)** — Add WIS modifier to initiative rolls. First turn of combat: +10 ft movement, one extra attack as part of the Attack action, that extra attack deals an additional 1d8 damage.
- **Umbral Sight (Gloom Stalker)** — Darkvision extended to 90 ft. While in darkness, invisible to any creature that relies on darkvision to see you.
- **Fey Ancestry (Half-Elf)** — Advantage vs charm, immune to magical sleep.
